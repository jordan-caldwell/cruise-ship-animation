Cruise Ship Loading Animation

Base Image:

This is the starting point for the animation. I found a boat svg from SVG Repo and used Illustrator to strip out the existing background elements. From there I added the waves, sky, and smoke clouds.

Objectives:

  • Simulate smoke clouds
    • Simulate a smoke cloud by animating opacity and position
    • Animate the two different stacks independently
  • Animate boat movement
    • Make the boat seem like its rocking by animating it up and down
  • Loop the waves
    • Simulate the waves rolling back and forth

Smoke Animation: Step 1

Use a basic fade out and up animation on every instance of the smoke cloud.

Smoke Animation: Step 2

Differentiate the stacks by changing their respective animation duration. Animate the smaller clouds and larger clouds separately.

Boat Animation:

Use a combination of rotate and translate to make the boat seem like its rocking up and down.

Wave Animation:

Make the waves roll by translating on the X axis. (Note: The wave exceeds the circle area and is chopped off by a clipping mask.

Completed Animation:

Putting each animation together results in the above animation.
